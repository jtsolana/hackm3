About Us

Hackm3 was established in July 2017 as a venue for ethical hackers to express themselves through the art of hacking.

Our Mission

To share our knowledge and skills about cyber security in a way that everyone could easily understand.

Principles and Limitations

We promote ethical hacking activities i.e Capture The Flag

We are passionate about hacking as a beautiful, limitless and unique medium of expression.

We respect each other's way of expressing opinions and feelings.

We follow safety rules and proper ways of practicing our skills, in order not to put anybody in harm's way.
